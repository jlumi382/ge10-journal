CCS Jamming Sesh Day 3

August 21, 2024
Computer Center Lobby
1 pt

What?

On August 21, 2024, I attended the CCS Jamming Sesh Day 3, which took place at the Computer Center Lobby from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. The event aimed to bring together CCS students to sing, jam, and have fun during the Hibalag week festivities. As a spectator, I was there simply to enjoy the music and observe my fellow CCS students expressing themselves through singing.

So What?

As the name suggested, the event was a casual and laid-back jamming session, and it turned out to be just that. I attended on the third day out of curiosity and met several talented singers, including Clindon, a fellow CCS Freshman. The atmosphere was relaxed and enjoyable, reminding me of the importance of taking breaks and having fun. It was a great reminder that it's okay to unwind and let go sometimes.

Now What?

Reflecting on the experience, it strengthened my value of balance between work and relaxation. I learned that taking time for fun activities, such as this jamming session, is essential to maintaining well-being, and I plan to apply this by incorporating similar breaks into my schedule. The activity is beneficial as it fosters a sense of community and offers a way for students to express themselves creatively. If I were to join again, I should learn how to sing a few songs to increase my involvement.

How This Changed Me

Because of this activity, I am reminded of how important it is to find joy in the little things and to embrace the opportunities to relax and connect with others. This experience has shown me that having fun is just as valuable as working hard, and it’s something I’ll strive to incorporate more into my daily routine.
