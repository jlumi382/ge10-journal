CCS Acquaintance Party: Binary Echoes of the Shadows

September 28, 2024
Silliman University Elementary Grounds
1 pt

What?

On September 28, 2024, from 5:30 PM to 10:00 PM, I attended the CCS Acquaintance Party: Binary Echoes of the Shadows at the Silliman University Elementary Grounds. Organized by the College of Computer Studies, the event aimed to help CCS students, also known as Trojans, meet each other, build new friendships, and enjoy a night of fun activities. As a participant and spectator, I joined in the celebrations and witnessed how the event brought everyone closer together.

So What?

Initially, I thought the program would be boring for me since I’m not usually interested in parties. However, I ended up having a good time thanks to the various games like the scanf printf game and the Easter egg hunt, which provided lots of laughter and enjoyment. Although the idea of taking breaks isn’t new to me, this event reminded me of the importance of taking time to relax and have fun, especially when faced with a busy schedule. It’s easy to forget to rest, but this experience emphasized how beneficial it is to unwind and enjoy the moment.

Now What?

The party allowed me to strengthen my bonds with fellow Trojans as we shared laughs and memorable moments together. Moving forward, I plan to continue working closely with my peers as we navigate our journey here at Silliman University. This was not only a great opportunity to let loose but also a meaningful way to connect with others, making it far from a waste of time.

How This Changed Me

Because of this activity, I am more encouraged to join future events and be open to opportunities that bring people together. The fun and camaraderie I experienced at the acquaintance party showed me that even though I’m not usually into social events, participating in them can lead to meaningful connections and lasting memories. This has inspired me to get more involved and build even stronger relationships with my fellow Trojans.
