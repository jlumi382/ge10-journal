    Acquaintance Party

    August 19, 2024
    Hibalag Grounds
    1 pt

    What?

    On August 19, 2024, I attended the Acquaintance Party at the Hibalag Grounds, which started at 7:00 PM. I participated as a spectator, with the main objective of enjoying the party and possibly meeting new friends.

    So What?

    The party was lively, and I was impressed by the Hibalag Grounds after the Parade of Lights. The various stalls caught my eye, and I made friends with Kirby and Jermaine. Together with my new friends from CCS, we enjoyed some barbecue. Vern, another CCS freshman, kindly treated us when some of us were short on money, teaching me about camaraderie. Despite just meeting recently, we felt a bond and trust, knowing we would repay Vern’s kindness and support each other at Silliman.

    Now What?

    Without this experience, I wouldn’t have made friends with Jermaine and Kirby, nor would I have realized the importance of mutual support. I know I will continue to receive help from my friends and also give back when they need it. If I were to participate in this event again, I would try to conserve more energy so I could stay longer and enjoy more of the experience.

    How This Changed Me

    Because of this activity, I am now more confident that I can navigate my time at Silliman University effectively, knowing that the school provides support in so many areas. The services and resources presented have reassured me that I will have the help I need whenever I face difficulties, whether academic or personal.
