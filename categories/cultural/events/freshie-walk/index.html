Freshie Walk

August 15, 2024
Luce Auditorium Grounds - Amphitheatre
1 pt

What?

The Freshie Walk on August 15, 2024, at the Luce Auditorium and the Amphitheater, was a memorable event designed to welcome us freshmen to Silliman University. My fellow freshmen from all Colleges participated, along with the School Pastors, who were the main speakers, and the confederates who helped in arranging the event and making it possible.

So What?

During the event, I had the opportunity to develop my social skills and even made a new friend with the person seated next to me. My expectations for the event was far from what I initially had in mind. I thought we were just going to march and chant our yells starting from the Luce Auditorium until we finish one lap around the school. However, I was pleasantly surprised when we reached the Amphitheater, where something more significant awaited us. There were inspiring speeches from our School Pastors, and we were even given free notebooks, ballpens, and, to my delight, ice cream! The pastors emphasized the importance of not only trusting in our own abilities but also putting our faith in God. Their words left a lasting impression on me, strengthening my faith.

Now What?

This experience taught the importance of faith and friendship. I will see to it that I and my classmates will continue to strengthen our bond as we face the challenges of a college student. Without this activity, I would still have been a lost 18 year old boy with no social life in Silliman University. If I were to do this event again as a welcoming upperclassman, I'll make sure to eat all flavors of ice cream! Jokes aside, I would like to spend a bit more time on practicing the yell, so that there would be more time for the freshmen from each college to get to know each other, so that they will hopefully be less self-conscious, more connected and not shy to let their voices out when chanting the yell.

How This Changed Me

Because of this activity, I am more faithful to God and I’ve made my first friends at Silliman University. Friendships I hope will last a lifetime.
