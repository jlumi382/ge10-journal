    Parade of Lights

    August 19, 2024
    Hibbard Hall - Ravello Field
    1 pt

    What?

    On August 19, 2024, I attended the Parade of Lights at the Hibbard Hall. It was an event that took place at 6:00 PM. I participated as a spectator of the performance by the Silliman University Band.

    So What?

    Initially, I attended the event just to earn points for my GE 10A course, expecting another typical noisy parade like those in my province of Siquijor. However, I was pleasantly surprised by the inclusion of new brass and wind instruments that enhanced the experience. The performers' glowing batons were captivating as well. By the end, I found myself fully engaged in the parade.

    Now What?

    This experience changed my perspective on parades and similar gatherings. What I had once considered as mere noise, I now see as an important celebration of culture. The Parade of Lights made me realize that these events are more than just a source of noise. They are a way of bringing people together to celebrate shared traditions and values. From now on, I will continue to appreciate parades not only in Silliman, but also in my hometown and other places.

    How This Changed Me

    Because of this event, I am aware of the importance of celebrating cultural events and deepening the sense of community within an institution. I am now also able to enjoy these activities.
