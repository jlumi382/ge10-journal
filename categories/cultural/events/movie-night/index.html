Movie Night Day 1

August 20, 2024
ATA Hall
1 pt

What?

The Movie Night Day 1 event took place on August 20, 2024, at ATA Hall from 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM. The goal of the event was to relax and enjoy movies with fellow CCS students. I attended as a spectator, simply there to hang out and watch a few films.

So What?

My friend Daniel, a first-year BSIT student, invited me to a horror movie event, which isn't my favorite genre. I considered leaving but decided to stay since I paid the Php 20.00 entrance fee. Luckily, I found my friend David, a fellow BSCS student, and we had a great time laughing at ourselves during the intense scenes. However, I didn't sleep well that night in my boarding house. On the third day, I returned for a much lighter movie, Deadpool & Wolverine, which was a fun contrast to Gonjiam: Haunted Asylum.

Now What?

This experience reminded me of the importance of taking breaks and spending time for myself, even in the middle of a busy schedule. The Movie Night provided a perfect opportunity to unwind and also brought me closer to my new CCS friends. If I could do this event again, I’d definitely go back for the second day and not miss out on the fun.

How This Changed Me

Because of this activity, I am more aware of the importance of balancing work and relaxation. It’s easy to get caught up in responsibilities, but taking a few hours to enjoy yourself and bond with friends can make a big difference in your overall well-being. This event showed me that unwinding can be a valuable way to stay connected and refreshed.
