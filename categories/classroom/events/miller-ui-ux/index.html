    Miller Solutions Development Inc. Industry Lecture: "Designing Intuitive User Experience: Key UI/UX Strategies"

    August 22, 2024
    ATA Hall
    1 pt

    What?

    On August 22, 2024, I attended the Miller Solutions Development Inc. Industry Lecture on Designing Intuitive User Experience: Key UI/UX Strategies held at the College of Computer Science (CCS) in Silliman University. As a participant, I was eager to learn about User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) strategies.

    So What?

    Initially, I expected the program to be quite serious, given its funny on the tech industry. However, to my surprise, the lecture had a light-hearted atmosphere. Just like the previous alumni who presented at the Miller Industry Lecture on Web Development Trends, the speakers managed to make their PowerPoint presentations both informative and funny. I particularly enjoyed the interactive game where we identified subtle mistakes in UI designs. However, when it came to the hands-on task of designing a UI for an app similar to Google Maps, I found it quite challenging due to my lack of experience in UI design. My interests lie more in the behind-the-scenes aspects of programming.

    Now What?

    This experience has given me a deeper understanding of various UI and UX design techniques. It has also emphasized the importance of creating user-friendly programs if you want them to be widely adopted. I plan to carry these insights with me as I continue my journey in software development.

    How This Changed Me

    Because of this activity, I am more aware of the critical role that UI and UX play in the overall success of a program, and I am motivated to incorporate these principles into my future projects.
