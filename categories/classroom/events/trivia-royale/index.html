    Trivia Royale

    August 21, 2024
    ATA Hall
    2 pts

    What?

    On August 21, 2024, from 8:30 AM to 9:30 AM, I participated in the Trivia Royale event at ATA Hall. The objective of the session was to learn more about the tech industry and the College of Computer Science (CCS) at Silliman University. I was part of the working committee, specifically contributing to the Technicals Committee for this event.

    So What?

    I was initially very nervous since it was my first time working in this role. The night before, I kept overthinking how challenging my tasks might be. However, it turned out to be a simple job, as we were mainly sitting on the side operating the Kahoot quiz game. I also got the chance to bond with JAD, another Committee member assigned to the same event. The experience was both fun and enlightening, with players using unique and funny names in the game. The trivia covered interesting facts about the tech industry and our college, and I learned things I didn’t know before.

    Now What?

    This event made me realize the importance of familiarizing yourself with your environment and the path you are pursuing. By participating, I got to learn about the inside jokes in CCS, discover cool trivia about the school, and gain insight into the tech industry. Next time, I would love to join the event as a player.

    How This Changed Me

    Because of this activity, I am more confident in my ability to contribute to future events. Initially, I was anxious about my role in the Technicals Committee, but the experience taught me that sometimes things are simpler than they seem. I also realized how much I enjoy being part of a team, collaborating with others, and learning new things along the way. This event gave me a greater appreciation for both the tech industry and the college, and I look forward to taking on more roles in similar activities with newfound confidence.
