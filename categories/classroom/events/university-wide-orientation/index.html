    Silliman University

    University-Wide Orientation

    September 11, 2024
    Luce Auditorium
    1 pt

    What?

    On September 11, 2024, from 9:00 AM to 11:00 AM, I attended the University-Wide Orientation at the Luce Auditorium as a spectator. The objective of the event was to familiarize freshmen with the various services, facilities, and laws that Silliman University provides to ensure an efficient and safe learning environment.

    So What?

    Initially, I thought the orientation would be a simple and quick overview of what we need to do to succeed in college, but I was completely wrong. We were given handouts for the flow of the event, and it had so much in store for us freshmen. One of the most significant parts for me was learning about the Office of Student Services, which I had no idea existed before. I also appreciated the university's commitment to safety by informing us about anti-sexual harassment, safe spaces, anti-hazing, bullying, and drug testing policies. Additionally, the student government offers various organizations and events, which could interest many students, and there are essential services like medical assistance and the university library. The Tula-Dula performance was also a very fun and creative way to explain the university services. While there was a lot of information for me to take in, I believe this information will be very useful for us freshmen.

    Now What?

    Attending the orientation made me realize that Silliman University provides a wide array of resources for students to ensure a smooth academic journey. From academic advising to mental health support, the various services available are designed to assist students in navigating both their studies and personal challenges. The information I gained was invaluable and will definitely help me know where to turn when I face challenges that I cannot handle on my own. I appreciated the opportunity to meet faculty members and fellow students who shared their own experiences, further reinforcing the sense of community that Silliman fosters. Overall, attending the event was far from a waste of time; it was a pivotal moment that equipped me with the knowledge and confidence to make the most of my university experience.

    How This Changed Me

    Because of this activity, I am now more confident that I can navigate my time at Silliman University effectively, knowing that the school provides support in so many areas. The services and resources presented have reassured me that I will have the help I need whenever I face difficulties, whether academic or personal.
