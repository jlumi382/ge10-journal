    What?

    On September 29, 2024, at 5:30 AM, I participated in a coastal cleanup at Silliman Beach organized by the DOST Scholar's Association. This event aimed to contribute to a cleaner environment and to promote the importance of community work. As a participant, I joined other volunteers in removing litter and debris from the coast, fulfilling the purpose of the event.

    So What?

    I expected the coast to have more trash, but to my surprise, it wasn’t as bad as I thought. During the cleanup, I came across a glass bottle near a neighborhood area, and an elderly lady thanked me for taking it away. This small interaction was deeply satisfying and reminded me why I enjoy participating in these events. Moments like this motivate me to stay engaged in community service.

    Now What?

    The experience has further strengthened my desire to support the community. As a DOST Scholar, where our organization frequently engages in community initiatives, I am committed to participating and encouraging others to join us. This event not only helped the residents but also made a positive impact on the food stalls in the area by providing a cleaner environment.

    How This Changed Me

    Because of this activity, I am more inspired to continue contributing to community events. The joy of helping out and seeing the immediate effects of our efforts makes me want to be more involved in similar activities in the future. This event has reaffirmed my commitment to being an active member of the community and promoting environmental awareness.
