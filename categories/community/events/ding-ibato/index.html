Ding Ibato

November 13, 2024
Ravello Field
3 pts

What?

I was a co-organizer and the head of logistics for the Ding Ibato event, a dodgeball tournament aimed at raising funds for the Dumaguete Animal Sanctuary. Our efforts resulted in sufficient donations to provide supplies such as dog food and anti-fungal shampoo. As the head of logistics, my responsibilities included preparing materials like medals and certificates and securing the permit for the Ravello Field venue. This role gave me a deeper understanding of event organization and logistics.

So What?

This experience taught me the value of teamwork and collaboration in achieving a common goal. Each committee member contributed their skills, and it was rewarding to see the event run smoothly and make a tangible impact for the Dumaguete Animal Sanctuary. On a personal level, I felt connected to the players since part of my duties involved retrieving dodgeballs that went out of bounds. The combination of responsibility and involvement made the event exciting and fulfilling for me.

Now What?

Moving forward, I plan to use the organizational skills I developed from this event in future activities. I have a clearer understanding of the importance of thorough preparation and effective teamwork, which I can apply to other areas in my life. Additionally, this experience inspired me to participate in more events with charitable goals, as they provide both personal growth and opportunities to contribute meaningfully to the community.

How This Changed Me

Because of this activity, I am more confident in my ability to lead and collaborate in a team. I gained a greater appreciation for the hard work that goes into organizing events and the impact they can have on others. This experience also reminded me of the joy in contributing to a cause bigger than myself, motivating me to stay engaged in community service projects.
