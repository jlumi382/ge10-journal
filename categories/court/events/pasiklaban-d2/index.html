    Pasiklaban Day 2

    October 29, 2024
    Cimafranca Ballfield
    1 pt

    What?

    On the second day of Pasiklaban, I watched the fifth men's volleyball game between the Green Pythons and the Blue Dragons. Volleyball is my favorite sport, so I could deeply understand and appreciate the plays made by both teams. The match was incredibly close and intense, which was evident in the screaming and cheering of the audience. In the end, the Blue Dragons emerged victorious after an exciting and hard-fought game.

    So What?

    This game reminded me how much I love volleyball and how deeply I connect with the sport. Watching such an intense match made me admire the players’ endurance, teamwork, and strategic thinking. It also stirred a strong desire within me to recover fully from my varicocele surgery so I can jump and play volleyball without any physical constraints. This experience not only entertained me but also reignited my passion for the game.

    Now What?

    Moving forward, I am determined to focus on my recovery and physical well-being so I can play volleyball again. This event has also inspired me to study game strategies more seriously, as I realized the importance of not just playing but also understanding the nuances of the sport. I aim to apply these lessons when I get back on the court and to cherish every moment I spend playing volleyball.

    How This Changed Me

    Because of this activity, I am more motivated to recover fully and regain my ability to play volleyball without limitations. This game has strengthened my passion for volleyball and reminded me of the joy and inspiration the sport brings to my life. It has also taught me to value my health and to work towards making a strong comeback.
