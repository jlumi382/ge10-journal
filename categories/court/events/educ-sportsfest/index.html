    College of Education Sportsfest

    November 6, 2024
    Silliman University Gym
    1 pt

    What?

    I attended the College of Education Sportsfest by chance when I heard the event from my boarding house. It piqued my curiosity so I decided to check it out and was lucky to witness this exciting sportsfest. I explored different games, including volleyball, soccer, badminton, and basketball. Ultimately, I stayed to watch three volleyball matches, as volleyball is my favorite sport. The event brought back my enthusiasm and made me reflect on my recovery journey from surgery.

    So What?

    This event reminded me of how much I love volleyball and how special it is to me compared to other sports. While I have been playing table tennis during my recovery, it cannot compare to the joy and energy volleyball brings me. Watching the matches reignited my passion for the game and motivated me to focus on my recovery so I can play volleyball again without any physical limitations. It was an uplifting experience that reminded me of the importance of doing what you love.

    Now What?

    Now, I am more determined than ever to continue my recovery and get back to playing volleyball. This event also taught me to appreciate opportunities like this, even when they come unexpectedly. Moving forward, I plan to actively look for ways to stay connected with the sport and maintain my enthusiasm for it, whether through watching games, practicing, or playing when I am fully healed.

    How This Changed Me

    Because of this activity, I am more inspired to recover fully and return to playing volleyball with all my heart. It reminded me of how deeply I value the sport and how much joy it brings to my life. This experience also encouraged me to stay open to unexpected opportunities and to cherish the moments that reignite my passions.
