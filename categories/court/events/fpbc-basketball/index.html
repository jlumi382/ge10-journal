    FPBC Basketball Tournament

    December 8, 2024
    Daro Basketball Court
    1 pt

    What?

    I attended the FPBC Basketball Tournament held on December 8, 2024, at the Daro Basketball Court. Initially, I thought it would be a bit dull since only two teams registered for the event. However, I was surprised to find it quite entertaining, as the players, who I usually see as my fellow CCS students, performed admirably on the court, showcasing a side of themselves I hadn’t seen before.

    So What?

    This experience taught me not to underestimate events based on their scale or participant count. Watching the game made me realize how people can surprise you with their hidden talents. It also deepened my appreciation for basketball as a sport that requires skill and teamwork, regardless of the context in which it is played.

    Now What?

    Moving forward, I plan to approach future events with a more open mind and positive attitude. I also want to engage more with my peers outside of academic settings, as these opportunities reveal their diverse talents and personalities. This might even inspire me to explore activities I wouldn’t normally participate in.

    How This Changed Me

    Because of this activity, I am more willing to give every event a fair chance and appreciate the talents of people around me. It showed me the value of stepping out of my usual routine and being open to discovering unexpected joys in seemingly ordinary experiences.
