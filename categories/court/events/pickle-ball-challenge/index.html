    All-Dumaguete Pickleball Challenge

    November 30, 2024
    Quezon Park
    1 pt

    What?

    I attended the All-Dumaguete Pickleball Challenge, though I arrived late due to having the wrong schedule. Despite this, I was fortunate to catch the final doubles game. It was a mixed match featuring two young men, a middle-aged woman, and an adult woman. Their skills and teamwork were inspiring to watch, highlighting the accessibility and inclusivity of pickleball as a sport.

    So What?

    Watching the game helped me appreciate how pickleball brings people of different ages and backgrounds together. It emphasized that sports are not only about competition but also about building connections and fostering inclusivity. Seeing players with such varied experiences perform well gave me a new perspective on teamwork and resilience.

    Now What?

    After witnessing this event, I am eager to try pickleball in our PE classes. It seems like a fun and engaging sport that combines skill and strategy. I plan to explore it more actively and potentially encourage others to give it a try. This experience has also reinforced my belief in trying new activities to expand my skills and interests.

    How This Changed Me

    Because of this activity, I am more open to exploring sports outside of my comfort zone. The inclusivity and skill displayed in the game I watched inspired me to embrace new opportunities and challenges, whether in sports or other areas of my life. I look forward to trying pickleball and growing from the experience.
