    Pasiklaban Day 1

    October 28, 2024
    Cimafranca Ballfield
    1 pt

    What?

    During Pasiklaban Day 1, I watched the second game of men's basketball between the Green Pythons and the Red Phantoms. As someone who used to play basketball as a kid, I didn’t enjoy the sport as much as volleyball, and I stopped pursuing it. Watching this game, however, was a completely different experience. The skill and technique displayed by the players were incredible, and their level of play was truly impressive. The Green Pythons won, and it was a well-deserved victory.

    So What?

    Seeing the game made me realize how little I know about the technical side of basketball and how much strategy is involved. It changed my perspective and gave me a newfound respect for the sport. It’s not just about scoring or running up and down the court—it requires precision, teamwork, and quick decision-making. The way the players communicated and executed their plays was inspiring.

    Now What?

    After this event, I’m motivated to look into basketball techniques and strategies, even if just to appreciate it better as a spectator. While volleyball will always be my preferred sport, this experience taught me to respect all sports for the dedication and skill they require. It also encouraged me to be open to learning more about activities outside of my comfort zone.

    How This Changed Me

    Because of this activity, I am more appreciative of sports I don’t actively play. Watching this basketball game has helped me develop respect for the skills and hard work required to excel in sports I don’t fully understand. It also reminded me to always stay open-minded and value the diversity of talents people bring to different areas.
