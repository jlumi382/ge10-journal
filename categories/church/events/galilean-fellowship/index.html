    Galilean Fellowship

    November 19, 2024
    Ausejo Hall
    1 pt

    What?

    On November 19, 2024, I attended the Galilean Fellowship event at Ausejo Hall during the University Christian Life Emphasis Month (UCLEM) at Silliman University. The event focused on the theme Faith Over Fear: Facing Our Giants, encouraging participants to confront their fears through faith in God. It included worship, discussions, and teachings that made the message of faith relatable.

    So What?

    The name Galilean Fellowship reflects the significance of Galilee in Jesus' ministry. The theme resonated with me, highlighting the daily struggles we face. Singing fostered unity as we shared our fears and definitions of faith. Stories from David and Goliath, along with Romans 8:37-39, reminded me that nothing can separate us from God's love and that true faith means trusting Him, even in difficult times.

    Now What?

    This event prompted me to reflect on how fear has influenced my life. I learned that faith in God is an active decision to trust Him, especially when faced with challenges. Acknowledging my “giants” involves facing them with faith, and the sense of community made this experience meaningful.

    How This Changed Me

    Because of this event, I am reminded that, despite my fears, God’s love is greater and His promises are sure.
